About me

I'm Nwarienne Michael Chukwudi, a dedicated, optimistic, and highly skilled professional with a diverse background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. My commitment to excellence drives me to consistently exceed expectations in every task I undertake. I bring over 5 years of experience, contributing significantly to the success of the organizations I've been a part of.

Professional Journey

I started my career as an Electrical Engineering Technologist, evaluating electrical installations and systems to isolate faults and implement corrective actions. I performed maintenance procedures and repairs on existing electrical products and systems, gaining hands-on experience in electrical engineering and troubleshooting.

As a Physics and Mathematics teacher at Bubendorff Memorial Grammar School Adazi-Nnukwu, I achieved significant milestones. I equipped students with the skills to understand complex mathematical concepts and solve physics problems. My teaching approach fostered a positive and engaging learning environment.

During my tenure as the Head of the Electrical Department at St. Anthony's Catholic Comprehensive Institute (SACCI), I achieved noteworthy milestones. I equipped over 1000 students with the skills to read electrical drawings, install electrical equipment, and troubleshoot existing ones. Collaborating with the IT department, I also resolved networking issues, contributing to a holistic learning experience for the students.

In my current role as the Head of Broadcasting Engineering at Group8 Africa, I oversee the operations of one of our branches. Successfully implementing state-of-the-art broadcasting technologies, optimizing workflow efficiency, and collaborating with cross-functional teams, I ensure high-quality broadcasting services. By streamlining production processes and enhancing broadcast quality, I contribute to the seamless transmission of programs to viewers.

Cybersecurity Transition

My transition into Cybersecurity began on 7th April 2021, with an intensive 7-month training at Cybersafe Foundation. Specializing in penetration testing, I've learned how to perform automated and manual penetration testing on enterprise networks and web applications using standard tools such as Metasploit, Nmap, Nessus, Burp Suite, etc.

Web Development Journey

I am a dedicated and forward-thinking software development professional with a strong foundation in both front-end development and IT automation with Python. My commitment to excellence is demonstrated by my completion of Meta Front-End Developer and IT Automation with Python courses on Coursera. I have a proven track record of contributing to the success of organizations through my technical skills and collaborative approach. Educator and Mentor
